About NOH3

Welcome to NOH3, home of the New Orleans Hash House Harriers. Here you will find everything you need to take part in the New Orleans chapter of the world's largest "drinking club with a running problem." We meet every week of the year to run a 3 to 5 mile trail through the great city of New Orlean's streets, alleys and parks. Afterwardss we conduct an entertaining ceremony known as "the circle" where we enjoy cold beer and some good laughs. We're a very social group. Our members come from all walks of life, all age groups (21+) and all abilities of running from slow to fast. Our one and only goal is to have fun! Welcome and On-On!

New to Hashing?

Hashing is a social running activity where a group follows marked trails for fun and camaraderie. It combines running, navigation, and post-run celebrations. The trails can be in urban or rural areas and cater to different fitness levels. Hashing is inclusive and a greate way to meet new people while enjoying a run.
