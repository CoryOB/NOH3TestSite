Facts, Answers and Questions

A brief list of factoids to make your time at NOH3 funner and easier

When are NOH3 hashes?

Most of our hashes are on Mondays but can be on Sudays and for special occasions Saturdays. For a list of all upcoming hashes please click HERE .

How old to I have to be to attend?

We request all partispents be at least 21 years old.

Can I bring my kids?

We request you leave the kids at home. This is an adult only event.

How long are the trails?

Typical trails range from three to five miles.

Do I have to be a runner to enjoy hashing?

No. NOH3 has both a running and a walking group.

How much does it cost?

The cost is $8 per trail run. This includes a beer for the trail to run or walk and a snack at the end.

What do I need to bring?

It's advisable to bring appropriate running attire, comfortable shoes, and a water bottle. Additionally, some hashes may require you to bring a shistle or a headlamp, depending on the trail conditions.

What is with all the weirdness?

Hashing has developerd its own quirky and humorous traditions over time, suck as singing bawdy songs, using playful nicknames, and engaging in drinking rituals. These traditions add to the fun comaraderie of the hashing experiance, creating a disting and sometimes "weird" atmosphere.
